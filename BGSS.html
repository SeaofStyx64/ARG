B.G.S.S Information File

The B.G.S.S, or Bureau of Global Security and Strategy, is a government body designed for the creation and implementation of security measures when regarding important government artifacts.

Recently, the B.G.S.S have been performing tests on chosen subjects, to get an accurate reading on their intelligence and problem solving skills.

They go under an alias known as the Birthday Gift Solving Service, a company designed to help subjects obtain a reward from completing the tests, adding incentive.

The subjects are chosen based off of age, intelligence, hobbies and skill.

However, due to the secretive nature of the B.G.S.S, all test subjects that fail mysteriously go missing, likely at the hands of the B.G.S.S themselves.

When a subject passes the test, their names and information are recorded, and the subject is considered for future employment.

The B.G.S.S are not what they seem.
